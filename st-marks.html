St. Mark's Square

Published on April 12, 2026

Saint Marks Square is a popular square in venice and is also known as the Piazza San Marco, with many famous buildings and historical events. The famous ones include the Doge's palace, the Saint Marks basilica and also the famous Caffe Florian.

The Saint Marks Square has been known for public events and ceremonies, and is also known for its occasional flooding called the "acqua alta" where water will temporarily cover parts of the square.

Today, the Saint Marks Square is one of the most memorable parts of venice, as you can go see the Square from the Saint Marks Basilica balcony, which allows beautiful views, especially at night when the lights turn on.
