San Gimignano - Tuscany

Published on April 1, 2026

San Gimignano is a small medieval town located in Tuscany, siena and was around during the 12th-13th century. It is known for its iconic stone towers and the beautiful valleys around it.

The city was important to travelers because they would stop by and would get food, a place to sleep, and could even do roadside trading sometimes to trade for valueble items.

As of today, the city is officially recognized as an UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city is known for the large amounts of tourists and university/college students traveling to study.
