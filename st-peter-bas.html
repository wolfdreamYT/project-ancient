Saint Peter's Basilica

Published on April 12, 2026

Saint Peter's Basilica is considered the biggest church in the world, and contains masterpieces such as the creation of adam. The church is located in the Vatican City and is where the pope lives.

The Saint Peter's Basilica was built over the tomb of Saint Peter, who was one of the 12 apostles of jesus Christ. Saint Peter was also the first ever pope, and is engraved in religious history. Inside the basilica is the famous michelangelo Pieta, which gains thousands of tourists daily.

The Saint Peter's Basilica began its construction in 1506 and was completed in 1626, with several famous architects who contributed to the design. This included Bramante, Michelangelo, Carlo Maderno, who all worked on parts of the church.

Data
